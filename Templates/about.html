About Us

Welcome to EnsoQuest

EnsoQuest is Ensono’s platform for trainee assessments — designed to make weekly tests simple, secure, and effective. With smart tools for test-taking, performance tracking, and study resources, it keeps learning on track.

EnsoQuest — Making Better Happen.

How It Works

We make assessments streamlined and stress-free:

  • Create your personalized account and get started.
  • Take tests that reward progress and consistency.
  • Receive instant results and monitor your performance.
  • Keep track of your attendance.
  • Export detailed performance and attendance reports for personal records or analysis.
  • Explore and contribute blogs to share knowledge and insights.
  • Download essential study materials and resources.
  • View important notices and announcements in one place.
  • Submit feedback to help enhance the training.
  • Engage in meaningful discussions with peers and mentors.
  • Reach out to the admin team easily through the Contact Us section.
  • Stay informed with real-time notifications and announcements.

Our Story

Our batch with our trainer, Mr. Burhan Malik, the inspiration behind our journey to create this platform.

It all began with a simple need and a shared vision

We are Anuja and Rajshree, good friends and college mates who were fortunate to be placed in the same company. During our training, we realized the need for a unified platform to streamline the test-taking process. This platform would allow users to take tests, track their results and performance, access resources, receive important notices and notifications, and provide valuable feedback.

Inspired and encouraged by our trainer, Mr. Burhan Malik, we decided to turn this idea into reality, and that's how this test application came to life.

EnsoQuest is more than just a tool. It's a journey of collaboration, creativity, and commitment to making learning better for everyone.
