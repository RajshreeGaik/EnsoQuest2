About Us

Welcome to EnsoQuest

EnsoQuest is Ensono’s platform for trainee assessments — designed to make weekly tests simple, secure, and effective. With smart tools for test-taking, performance tracking, and study resources, it keeps learning on track. EnsoQuest — Making Better Happen.

How It Works

EnsoQuest provides a streamlined experience for trainees and trainers to manage weekly assessments with ease:

  • Sign up and create your personalized account.
  • Access scheduled assessments.
  • Download study materials and resources.
  • Take weekly tests through a secure and user-friendly interface.
  • Get instant results and track your performance over time.
  • Provide feedback on your training experience each week.
  • Receive updates, notices, and important announcements directly on the platform.
